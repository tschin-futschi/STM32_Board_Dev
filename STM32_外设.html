SysTick = HCLK = 180 MHz  →  Reload = 180000 - 1  →  中断周期 = 1 ms
PLL 计算公式：
VCO_input = HSE / PLL_M = 8 / 8 = 1 MHz
VCO_output = VCO_input × PLL_N = 1 × 360 = 360 MHz
SYSCLK = VCO_output / PLL_P = 360 / 2 = 180 MHz
